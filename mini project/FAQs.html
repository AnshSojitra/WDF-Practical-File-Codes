Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Game Fusion.

How do I buy a game? ▼

You can browse our catalog, select a game, and click "Buy Now". Payments can be made through credit card, debit card, or PayPal.

Can I refund a purchased game? ▼

Yes, refunds are available within 14 days of purchase if the game has been played for less than 2 hours. Contact support for help.

Do you offer discounts or sales? ▼

We run seasonal sales and weekly discounts on select games. Keep an eye on our Events page for updates.

Can I access my games on multiple devices? ▼

Yes, once purchased, your games are linked to your Game Fusion account and can be played on any compatible device.

How do I contact customer support? ▼

You can reach us via the Contact page by submitting a support request, or email us at support@gamefusion.com.
